PROCESS

Inspiration

After conducting user research and mentally conceptualizing what was learned, I put pencil to paper to create some low-fidelity prototypes in the form of rough sketches. This process allows me to validate or discredit ideas quickly with little time and effort.

Ideation

Ideation occurs in the form of wireframes, user flows, information architecture, and higher-fidelity interactive prototypes. I love this phase of the design process because it is when the project starts to take shape and when some real ah-ha moments occur.

Implementation

Implementation is hardly the end of a project. After a site goes live, it is live-tested, tracked and iterated upon to ensure the user experience is continually improving.
