Project Background

I volunteer my time to help the non-profit Winter Wildlands with issues or changes on their Wordpress site. I built the microsite above in response to a request to update the look of the Film Festival pages on the organization's main website.

The worldwide film festival is a major event for the non-profit, and I felt a microsite would better serve the organization for several reasons:

  • the ability to implement a friendly URL
  • better SEO through targeted content
  • responsive layout
  • the design could be more fun to match the energy of the film festival

The site prototype is in evaluation for implementation.
