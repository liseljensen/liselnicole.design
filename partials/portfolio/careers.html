Project Background

The Simplot Careers site project started as a request from Human Resources to update the existing Careers pages on the main corporate website. There were two reasons for the request: 1) to make the pages appeal to millennials, and 2) to reduce the number of emails from users asking how to apply.

After conducting user research and assessing the constraints of the main website, I suggested the Careers section be broken out into a separate microsite and pitched the design above. Creating a new site allowed the incorporation of compelling images, a bright color scheme, and a long scroll layout that was more mobile-friendly — all important aspects of appealing to millennials according to research.

Since the launch of the new site:

  • the number of frustrated user emails dropped dramatically
  • the site bounce rate decreased 25%
  • page views increased 36%
  • over half of the exit traffic occurs on the Apply Now page — indicating a high conversion rate to the Careers portal to apply
